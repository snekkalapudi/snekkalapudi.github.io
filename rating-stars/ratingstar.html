Simple scalable rating star

I think this is one of the simplest way to deal with rating stars in 2011.

This is all you would require for handling rating stars from 0.0 - *

Image used here is: 

<style type="text/css">
 .ratingStars,.ratingStars div{
  width:60px;
  height:12px;
  background:url('star.png') repeat-x;
}
.ratingStars div{
  background-position: 0 -13px; 
}
</style>

<div class="ratingStars">
  <div style="width:59px"></div>
</div>

Here each star is of 12px wide. So, 5 stars => 12px*5 = 60px So, if the rating is 4.0, then star is 4.0*12px = 48px and so on ...

Thanks to Steven Whyte, my colleague @Walmart.com with whom, I was having an conversation about rating stars and this is the final outcome.
